About

MUSIC is a 3+1D relativistic second-order viscous hydrodynamics for heavy ion collisions

  • 2009-2010

    Our Humble Beginnings

    MUSIC was born at McGill as an ideal (3+1)D hydrodynamic code.

  • October 2010

    (3+1)D Viscous hydrodynamics takes off

    The first (3+1)D event-by-event viscous hydrodynamic code come out

  • 2013-2015

    Debut of the full nonlinear viscous hydrodynamics

    A full set of second order viscous non-linear coupling terms is implemented in MUSIC
